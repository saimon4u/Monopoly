Game Rules

Maximum Players

How many players can join the game

Vacation Cash

If a player lands on Vacation, all collected money from taxes and bank payments will be earned.

Auction

If someone skips purchasing the property landed on, it will be sold on the highest bidder.

x2 rent on a full-set properties

If a player owns a full property set, the base rent payment will be doubled.

Collect rent while in prison

Rent will be collected when landing on properties whose owners are in prison.

Mortgage

Mortgage properties to earn 50% of their cost, but you won't get paid rend when players land on them.

Even build

Houses and hotels must be built up and sold off evenly within a property set.

Starting Cash

Adjust how much money players start the game with

Randomize player order

Randomly reorder players at the beginning of the game
